Korean Turkey

Description

Perfect for any bulk with its high protein and calories, and so good it'll stop you from those pesky cheat days!

The sweet tang will be sure to have you coming back for more, along with the ease of preparation.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3/4 cup uncooked rice
  • 1 head broccoli
  • green onions for garnish

Turkey

  • 3/4 lb lean ground turkey
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon ginger

Sauce

  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/8 â…› teaspoon pepper

Steps

  1. Cook rice according to package directions. Portion out into four 2-cup capacity meal prep containers and allow to cool.
  2. Shake up all sauce ingredients and set aside.
  3. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the broccoli and cook for 5 or so minutes, until slightly softened. Remove from heat and divide between meal prep containers.
  4. Add turkey to pan. Cook, breaking it up with a spatula, for 5-8 minutes, until completely cooked through and no longer pink.
  5. Make a space in the middle of the pan. Add the garlic and ginger and cook for 1 minute, stirring up a bit.
  6. Give the sauce a shake up and pour over the ground turkey and garlic/ginger. Stir until everything is mixed through, about 1-2 minutes. Remove from heat.
  7. Divide turkey/sauce mixture between the meal prep containers. Sprinkle with green onions if desired.
